# papers-in-15-minutes

Trending AI papers, simplified. Every day we pick the most interesting papers from arXiv and Hacker News and break them down so you can read each one in about 15 minutes.

No jargon walls. No 40-page PDFs. Just the ideas that matter, explained clearly.

## How it works

Each paper is processed through [PaperGlide](https://paperglide.net), which rewrites dense academic papers into structured, readable summaries. The "Read" link takes you to an interactive reader with section-by-section breakdowns.

Papers are selected daily based on:
- Trending on Hacker News or Reddit
- New on arXiv in ML/AI/Stats
- Scored by a combination of community engagement and predicted relevance
